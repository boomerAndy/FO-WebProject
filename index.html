Who is Frank Ocean?

Frank Ocean is an American songwriter, record producer and fashion designer from Louisiana. He is best known for his work on his grammy award winning record 'Channel Orange' and decade defining surprise drop of 2016, 'Blonde', which was also ranked #1 most important album of the last 10 years by Pitchfork late last year.
